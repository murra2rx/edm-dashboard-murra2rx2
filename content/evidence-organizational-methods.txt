
## LOGIC MODEL

**X (Independent Variable):** Inconsistent Manager Guidance/Training in Hybrid Work Environments

**M (Mediator):** Manager Feedback Frequency [Not directly measured in available datasets]

**Y (Dependent Variable):** Employee Performance Outcomes (Engagement, Turnover, Productivity)

---

## RESEARCH QUESTION

How does manager guidance consistency and training availability relate to organizational performance outcomes (employee engagement, turnover, productivity) in hybrid and remote work environments?

---

## DATA REPOSITORY 1: Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS)

### Dataset 1: BLS OEWS - Human Resources Managers (SOC 11-3121)

**URL:** https://www.bls.gov/oes/current/oes113121.htm  
**Type:** National Occupational Employment and Wage Statistics  
**Date:** May 2024 (most recent data available)

**Variables Available:**
- Total Employment (nationwide): Number of HR managers across all industries
- Employment by Industry: Professional/Technical Services, Finance/Insurance, Healthcare, Manufacturing, Information Technology, etc.
- Median Annual Wage: Salary data for HR/manager compensation benchmarking
- Percentile Wage Estimates: 10th, 25th, 75th, 90th percentile ranges
- State-Level Employment: Geographic distribution of management roles
- Metropolitan Area Data: HR manager employment in major metro areas

---

### Dataset 2: BLS OEWS Historical Tables (2014-2024)

**URL:** https://www.bls.gov/oes/tables.htm  
**Timeframe:** 10-year longitudinal data (2014-2024)

**Variables Available:**
- Year-over-year employment changes: HR managers, Training managers, First-line supervisors
- Employment growth rates: Tracks demand for management/training roles
- Industry sector trends: Which sectors growing/declining in management employment
- Wage trends over time: Real wage growth adjusted for inflation
