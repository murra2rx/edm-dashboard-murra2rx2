# Identify and analyze all parties affected by your problem and solution

### Middle Management
Interest in Problem: Managers struggle with unclear expectations and inconsistent processes, leading to increased 
workload and difficulty assessing remote employees fairly.
Interest in Solution: Training and standardized tools make evaluations easier, reduce stress, and improve team
performance.
Influence Level: Medium-High
Support Level: Likely supporter (solution reduces burden and improves outcomes)

### Employees/Staff
Interest in Problem: Remote and hybrid employees lack clarity about expectations, feel uncertain about performance,
and may miss development opportunities.
Interest in Solution: Consistent feedback increases motivation, clarity, fairness, and support for growth.
Influence Level: Medium
Support Level: Strong supporter

### Other Internal Groups
- **Department/Group:** Human Resources 
Interest in Problem: HR cannot maintain consistent performance standards across teams, making it hard to track
trends or ensure fairness.
Interest in Solution: Standardization improves compliance, reduces disputes, and provides clean performance data.
Influence Level: High
Support Level: Key driver / strong supporter

## External Stakeholders

### Customers/Clients
Interest in Problem: Inconsistent employee performance affects service quality, response times, and customer satisfaction.
Interest in Solution: A more reliable, consistent workforce leads to better service and fewer delays.
Influence Level: Medium
Support Level: Likely supporter

### Suppliers/Partners
Interest in Problem: Inconsistent team performance leads to communication delays and unpredictability in collaboration.
Interest in Solution: More consistent execution improves coordination and strengthens partnership reliability.
Influence Level: Low-Medium
Support Level: Indirect support

### Regulatory Bodies
Interest in Problem: Poor documentation or uneven performance practices can create compliance risks, especially in 
regulated industries.
Interest in Solution: Standardized processes help demonstrate fairness, transparency, and compliance with labor
and evaluation standards.
Influence Level: High
Support Level: Neutral but supportive

### Community/Society
Interest in Problem: Inconsistent performance feedback contributes to employee stress and burnout, which negatively 
affects families and community wellbeing.
Interest in Solution: A healthier remote work environment supports mental health and work-life balance.
Influence Level: Medium
Support Level: Broad supporter

## Stakeholder Engagement Strategy

### High Influence, High Interest (Manage Closely)
Senior Management: Frequent updates, data summaries, involvement in decision-making
HR Department: Co-design policies, co-lead rollout, regular implementation meetings

### High Influence, Low Interest (Keep Satisfied)
Regulatory Bodies: Provide compliance documentation and periodic updates
Some Middle Managers: Ensure processes are easy to adopt and require minimal extra work

### Low Influence, High Interest (Keep Informed)
Employees: Clear communication, surveys, feedback loops, Q&A sessions
Community: Publish wellness initiatives or work-culture improvements

### Low Influence, Low Interest (Monitor)
Suppliers/Partners: Provide updates only if changes affect timelines or communication
Clients: Monitor service quality and communicate only when necessary

## Key Stakeholder Insights for Evidence Collection

### Who to Interview/Survey
- Remote and hybrid employees
- Middle managers who conduct evaluations
- HR staff responsible for performance systems
- Senior leadership who use performance data


### What Questions to Ask
Employees:
- Do you clearly understand your performance expectations?
- How consistent is the feedback you receive?

Managers:
- What challenges do you face when evaluating remote employees?
- How confident are you in giving structured feedback?

HR:
- How inconsistent are evaluations across teams?
- What gaps exist in tools or training?

Leadership:
- How does inconsistent performance data affect decision-making?

### How to Engage
- Online surveys for employees
- Small focus groups with managers
- One-on-one interviews with HR and senior leadership
- Review of performance documentation and evaluation patterns
